The lions

Present in Africa and India, the lion typically inhabits grasslands and savannahs, but is absent in defence forest. It is usually more diurnal than other big cats but when persecuted adapts to being active at night and at twilight. A lion pride consists of a few males, related females and cuds. Prides vary in size and composition from three to 20 adult lions, depanding on habit andprey availablity. Females cooparate when huntingand prey mostly on large ungulates, including antelope, deer, buffalo, zebra and even giraffe.
